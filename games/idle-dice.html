Idle Dice is a great game for when you feel like playing a game without putting a ton of effort. While you will likely do better if you keep a close eye on the game so you can make upgrades when necessary, there is no shame in putting Idle Dice on another tab and checking in on occasion while you are doing something else. That’s the beautiful thing about idle games - they don’t need all of your love and attention in order to succeed in them.
